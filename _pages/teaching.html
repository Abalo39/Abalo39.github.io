--- title: "Mathematics and Physics Teacher" collection: teaching type: "High School Level" permalink: /teaching/2019-kara-togo venue: "High Schools in Kara" date: 2019-01-01 location: "Kara, Togo" --- Delivered high school–level instruction in mathematics and physics (2019–2023), focusing on conceptual understanding and systematic problem-solving. **Key Mathematics Modules:** * **Analysis:** Continuity, Differentiability, and Polynomial Functions. * **Algebra:** Complex Numbers, Arithmetic and Geometric Sequences. * **Probability:** Combinatorics and Logical Reasoning. * **Geometry:** Spatial (Three-Dimensional) Geometry. **Key Physics Modules:** * **Mechanics:** Kinematics, Free Fall, and Motion under Gravity. * **Electricity:** Alternating Current, RLC Circuits, and Oscillations. * **Thermodynamics:** Ideal Gases and Thermodynamic Processes. * **Waves:** Wave Phenomena and Propagation.
