--- title: "Mathematics and Physics Teacher" collection: teaching type: "High School Level" permalink: /teaching/2019-kara-teacher venue: "High Schools, Kara Region" date: 2019-01-01 location: "Kara, Togo" --- **Period:** 2019–2023 **Student Level:** High School (Grades 10–12) **Teaching Approach:** Focused on conceptual understanding, systematic problem-solving, and real-world applications. ## Course Overview Delivered comprehensive high school curriculum in Mathematics and Physics, preparing students for national examinations while fostering critical thinking and analytical skills. ### **Mathematics Curriculum** * **Analysis & Calculus:** * Limits, Continuity, and Differentiability * Polynomial Functions and their Applications * Introduction to Differential Equations * **Algebra & Number Theory:** * Complex Numbers and their Geometric Representation * Arithmetic and Geometric Sequences/Series * Matrix Operations and Systems of Equations * **Probability & Statistics:** * Combinatorics (Permutations, Combinations) * Probability Theory and Distributions * Logical Reasoning and Mathematical Proof * **Geometry & Trigonometry:** * Spatial (Three-Dimensional) Geometry * Trigonometric Functions and Identities * Coordinate Geometry and Conic Sections ### **Physics Curriculum** * **Mechanics:** * Kinematics: Motion in One and Two Dimensions * Newton's Laws of Motion and Applications * Free Fall and Projectile Motion under Gravity * Work, Energy, and Power * **Electricity & Magnetism:** * Alternating Current Circuits * RLC Circuits and Electrical Oscillations * Electromagnetic Induction and Applications * **Thermodynamics:** * Ideal Gas Laws and Kinetic Theory * Thermodynamic Processes (Isothermal, Adiabatic) * Heat Transfer Mechanisms * **Waves & Optics:** * Wave Phenomena and Propagation * Sound Waves and Acoustics * Geometric Optics and Lens Systems * **Modern Physics:** * Introduction to Quantum Concepts * Nuclear Physics and Radioactivity ## Teaching Philosophy & Impact * **Active Learning:** Implemented hands-on experiments and interactive problem-solving sessions * **Individualized Support:** Provided additional tutoring for struggling students and enrichment for advanced learners * **Assessment Design:** Created comprehensive evaluation tools including quizzes, exams, and practical assessments * **Curriculum Development:** Adapted teaching materials to local context while maintaining international standards * **Student Outcomes:** Consistently achieved above-average student performance in national examinations * **STEM Advocacy:** Encouraged female students to pursue mathematics and physics through targeted mentorship
